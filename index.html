How it works

My Health in Your Hands is a mobile app that lets you book appointments and track queues in public hospitals. With our app, you can:

  • Find the best hospital for your needs based on location, availability, and queue status
  • Secure your spot and receive reminders and notifications about your appointment
  • Save time and avoid long waiting times in the registration process
  • Access your medical records and prescriptions online
  • Rate your experience and provide feedback to improve the quality of service

Our app is easy to use and works on both iOS and Android devices. All you need is a smartphone and an internet connection.

Our Story

Our journey with “My Health in Your Hands” isn’t just a development story; it's a profound exploration rooted in our shared vision. Each member of our team brings a unique perspective, but our collective connection to this project is woven from personal experiences and a deep desire to make a meaningful difference.

Coming from diverse backgrounds, we've witnessed firsthand the challenges individuals face in accessing timely healthcare due to endless queues. Memories of loved ones patiently waiting for hours in hospital lines have left indelible marks on each of us. It's not just about inconvenience; it's about the emotional toll it takes on patients and their families.

As a team, we came together with a shared purpose — to create “My Health in Your Hands” not just as a technical solution, but as a heartfelt response to alleviate the burden of waiting for individuals and families alike.

Throughout this collective journey, every line of code we've written is a step toward transforming those waiting moments into opportunities for individuals to focus on their well-being rather than the ticking clock. It has become our shared mission to contribute to a solution that resonates with the emotions we've all witnessed in our respective backgrounds.

So, when people ask, “Why this project?” it's not merely a fascination with healthcare technology; it's our commitment to the human stories, a dedication to making healthcare more accessible, and a tribute to the resilience of those who’ve patiently waited in queues.

In essence, “My Health in Your Hands” is more than an app; it's a collective manifestation of empathy, a technological embrace aimed at ensuring no one feels powerless in the face of healthcare queues. The hours spent coding weren’t just about algorithms; we were collectively weaving a narrative of compassion and understanding.

As we unveil “My Health in Your Hands” to the world, it’s not just a project launch; it's a heartfelt invitation to join us, the entire team, in redefining the healthcare experience. Together, let’s turn waiting moments into empowering strides toward a healthier, more compassionate future.
